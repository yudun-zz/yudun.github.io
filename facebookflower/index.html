About this Facebook Visualization

Hi, I am Shimin Wang, and this is a demo for my previous work on the visualization of personal Facebook data.

I am always interested in data visualization and frontend development. In the year of 2014, using D3.js, I designed three distinguished visualization for Personal Facebook data and performed a compact user study on these designs. I published this research work as the first Author in Computer Graphics and Applications, IEEE, 35(4), 82-93.
